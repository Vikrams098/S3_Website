Our Story

A cafe born from a love of good coffee

KV's Cafe opened its doors in 2018 as a 12-seat coffee bar in Indiranagar. Vikram started it with one espresso machine, a notebook of recipes, and a firm belief that a great cup of coffee can turn any morning around. Today we serve hundreds of guests daily — but every order still gets the same care as that first cup.
